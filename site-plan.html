LocalExplorer Project Site Plan

Site Name

LocalExplorer — This name was chosen to reflect the purpose of the website: helping users explore and discover hidden local gems, events, and businesses in their city.

Optional Domain: localexplorer.city

Site Purpose

The LocalExplorer website provides a platform where users can find and share hidden attractions, small businesses, local events, and must-visit spots in their area. It features a searchable business directory, a map of hidden gems, weather updates, and a user-submission form to recommend new places.

Scenarios

  • What are some unique places to visit in my city this weekend?
  • How can I recommend a great café or hidden bookstore I found recently?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #004e64 (Used for headers, nav background, and main elements)
  • Accent Color: #00a5cf (Used for buttons, links, and highlights)
  • Background: #f4f4f4 (Used for page background)

Typography

  • Urbanist: Headings and section titles (modern and bold)
  • Roboto: Body text and forms (clean and readable)
